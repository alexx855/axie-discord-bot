Privacy Policy for Axie Discord Marketplace Bot

Last updated: August 27, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Axie Discord Marketplace Bot (the "Bot") privacy policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Discord bot.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of the Bot. The Bot operates within the Discord platform and does not store or process personal data unless explicitly provided by the user during interaction.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use it for any purpose. The Bot primarily provides functionalities within the Discord platform and does not retain or process user data.

4. Cookies

The Bot does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is essential to us. However, as we do not collect or store personal data, we do not have specific data security measures in place. Discord, the platform on which the Bot operates, is responsible for the security of data transmitted through their services.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Axie Discord Marketplace Bot's privacy practices, please visit our GitHub repository at https://github.com/alexx855/axie-discord-bot/ for contact information.
